Our Services

Group tutoring

In group tutoring sessions, students are placed in groups with up to 7 people that are in the same class, working on similar topics. Topics that each student is struggling on will be covered in group wide whiteboard reviews.

Private tutoring

In our 1-on-1 private tutoring, the student comes with topics they are struggling with and each topic will be covered in depth within the session.
